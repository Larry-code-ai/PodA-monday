How metrics are defined. REP = enrolled now & next-term status “Not Active.” Enrol % = re-enrolled ÷ (re-enrolled + REP) for currently enrolled students. Leaderboard flags. Low contact = REP ≥ 10 and contact rate under 55% — dialling but not connecting. Thin cover = REP ≥ 10 and attempt coverage under 90% — students left with no call attempt in the window. Top = highest enrolment rate among advisors with a base of 40+.
Start target = incentive target grossed up by the partner drop-rate buffer (UTS 8%, JCU 13%, RMIT 10%, SCU 15%) — the number to aim for so drops don't leave you short.
